About — mp3.vinyl.rtf

Hi. We are mp3.vinyl.

We're a small, independent record shop running out of Kuala Lumpur and Penang. Started in 2024 on Xiaohongshu as a hobby — sharing records with other collectors here in Malaysia — and we've slowly grown from there.

We focus on what we know and love: contemporary hip-hop, R&B, and indie. Every record that comes through here is chosen, not stocked. No bulk, no random re-sellers, no filler.

Self-collection available in KL & Penang. Shipping across Malaysia. Looking for a specific pressing? DM us.
